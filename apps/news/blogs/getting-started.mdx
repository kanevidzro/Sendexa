## Welcome to Sendexa

Sendexa is a powerful email marketing and automation platform designed to help you engage with your audience effectively. Whether you're a small startup or an established business, Sendexa provides the tools you need to create, send, and track professional email campaigns.

<Callout type="tip" title="Quick Tip">
  Have Sendexa already? Jump straight to creating your first campaign by heading to the dashboard.
</Callout>

## Prerequisites

Before you get started, make sure you have the following:

- A Sendexa account (sign up at [sendexa.co](https://sendexa.co))
- A verified email domain or subdomain
- Basic understanding of email marketing concepts
- Access to your DNS settings (for domain verification)

## Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Visit [sendexa.co](https://sendexa.co) and click **"Get Started"**
2. Enter your email address and create a strong password
3. Check your email for a verification link and click it
4. Complete your profile information

<Callout type="info">
  We recommend using a business email address to keep your account professional and secure.
</Callout>

## Step 2: Verify Your Domain

To ensure maximum email deliverability, you need to verify your domain:

### Add DNS Records

Navigate to **Settings â†’ Domains** and follow these steps:

1. Click **"Add Domain"**
2. Enter your domain name (e.g., `mail.yourcompany.com`)
3. Copy the CNAME records provided
4. Log into your domain registrar (GoDaddy, Namecheap, etc.)
5. Add the CNAME records to your DNS settings
6. Return to Sendexa and click **"Verify Domain"**

<Note type="warning" hideIcon={false}>
  DNS propagation can take 24-48 hours. If verification fails, wait a bit and try again.
</Note>

### DNS Records You'll Need

| Record Type | Name | Value |
|------------|------|-------|
| CNAME | sendmail | sendmail.sendexa.co |
| TXT | dkim | v=DKIM1; p=YOUR_DKIM_KEY |
| TXT | _dmarc | v=DMARC1; p=quarantine; |

## Step 6: Monitor Your Results

After sending your campaign, track its performance:

### Key Metrics to Watch

| Metric | What It Means |
|--------|---------------|
| **Delivery Rate** | % of emails successfully delivered |
| **Open Rate** | % of recipients who opened the email |
| **Click Rate** | % of recipients who clicked a link |
| **Conversion Rate** | % of clicks that led to desired action |
| **Bounce Rate** | % of emails that failed to deliver |
| **Unsubscribe Rate** | % of people who opted out |

## Common Questions

<Callout type="warning" title="Why are my emails going to spam?">
  This can happen due to domain verification issues, poor sender reputation, or flagged content. Check your DKIM/SPF settings and review email content for spam triggers.
</Callout>

<Callout type="info" title="Can I schedule campaigns in advance?">
  Yes! When creating a campaign, select "Schedule" instead of "Send Now" to choose when your email should be delivered.
</Callout>

<Callout type="tip" title="What's the best time to send emails?">
  Generally, Tuesday-Thursday at 10 AM in your recipient's timezone performs well, but test different times to find what works for your audience.
</Callout>
